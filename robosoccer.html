ROBO Soccer

Problem statement :

Design and build two remote controlled robots (defender and goal keeper). The team with maximum goals at the completion of the match will emerge as the winner.


Rules

The team should not consist of more than four member. Each member from same point is mandatory. The robot should follow the robot specifications provided. Any deviation from the mentioned specifications will lead to disqualification. No test practice will be allowed on the main arena. AC supply will be provided in the college. The arena may subject to change before the commencement of any round. The structure of the robot should not be changed during the competition. The ball can either be pushed or dragged by the team.


Robo Specifications


The weight limit of the robot for this competition is 5 kg. Each robot should not exceed by maximum 30cmX30cmX30cm. The bot can either be wired or wireless. The maximum allowed voltage to power the robot is 12v.


Arena Specification


The playing field is 240cm by 480cm. The field is marked by a white line which is part of the playing field. The field has two goals, centered on each of the shorter sides of the playing field, the goal inner space is 60cm width, 30cm high and 30cm deep, box shaped. A center circle will be drawn on the field. It is 80cm in diameter. It is thin black marker line, In front of each goal there is a 50cm wide and 80cm long penalty area. The penalty areas are marked by a black line of 20mm width.


Difficulty level of hurdles will be increased.


Penalties


The participants must not touch the bot during the competition without taking a timeout, doing so will result in penalty. The participants must not touch the ball during the competition. If your bot damages the opponentâ€™s bot, then it will result in penalty.


Certification policies


Top two teams will be awarded by certification of excellence and cash prize for the event. All teams will be provided by certificate of participation. The winner has to complete the task, if the fail to complete the task then they will be not eligible for certificate.
