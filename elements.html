ROBO Race

The team has to design a manually operated wired robot capable to overcome obstacles or hurdles in the path within the shortest period of time. The team should not consist of more than 4 members. Each member from same college is mandatory. The robot should follow the robot specifications provided. Any deviation from the mentioned specifications will lead to disqualification.No test practice will be allowed on the main arena. AC supply will be provided in the college.The arena may subject to change before the commencement of any round.The structure of the robot should not be changed during the competition.If the Robot crosses a checkpoint, and moves off track, then the Robot would be placed back on the previous checkpoint crossed with penalty.

ROBO CANOEING

This is a racing event. Two robotic boats A and B have to start simultaneously from their starting points. So, fastest and most balanced robot will win. No offensive strategies are allowed. It might lead to disqualification. Each team can have maximum four members. Students from different institutes can form a team. Unethical behavior could lead to disqualification. Event-coordinators have all the rights to take final decision for any matter during the event. Boats can be build from toolkits. But, readymade boats are not allowed. Judge's decision will be considered final.
