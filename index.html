About Navyantram

_______

Welcome to the most electrifying competition and robotics event "NAVYANTRAM" at shri vaishnav vidyapeeth vishwavidyalaya. NAVYANTRAM is the most spectacular robotics event and competition at various levels. After the astonishing beginning of the competition in 2k19, we are all set and here again for the 2nd time with more zeal and zest. After the gigantic success of NAVYANTRAM 2k19 we are here with some more remarkable add on's like robo war and robo canoeing. This vibrant event unfolds many hair-raising opportunities like never before.
