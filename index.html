About Navyantram

_______

Welcome to the most electrifying competition and robotics event "NAVYANTRAM" at shri vaishnav vidyapeeth vishwavidyalaya. NAVYANTRAM is the most spectacular robotics event and competition at various levels. After the astonishing beginning of the competition in 2k19, we are all set and here again for the 2nd time with more zeal and zest. After the gigantic success of NAVYANTRAM 2k19 we are here with some more remarkable add on's like robo war and robo canoeing. This vibrant event unfolds many hair-raising opportunities like never before.

Major Events

information and listing of events.

Robo Race

The team has to design a manually operated wired robot capable to overcome obstacles or hurdles in the path within the shortest period of time.

Robo Soccer

Design and build two remote controlled robots (defender and goal keeper). The team with maximum goals at the completion of the match will emerge as the winner.

Robo Canoeing

Design a Wireless controlled Robotic-boat capable of floating on water and complete specified task within provided time. The rounds will be decided according to groups participated. The Robotic boat which gains maximum points will be the winner.

Robo War

Design a Wireless controlled Robot capable of fighting another oponent and complete specified task within provided time. The rounds will be decided according to groups participated. The Robot which gains maximum points will be the winner.

Line Follower

Design an autonomous wireless robot, capable of racing on a rough track along with its fellow bots following a black line on a white background in the shortest period of time.

FAQ

What are the Themes?
There are 5 themes in total:
  • Robo Race{Team of minimum 2 members and maximum 4 members }
  • Robo War { Team of minimum 2 members and maximum 4 members }
  • Robo Canoeing { Team of minimum 2 members and maximum 4 members }
  • Robo Soccer { Team of minimum 2 members and maximum 4 members }
  • Line Follower { Team of minimum 2 members and maximum 4 members }

How do i register myself?

On the homepage, register yourself. Then choose a theme or multiple themes that suits you the best.

After that make payment and you are registered for the event.


What is the eligibility criteria?

Both college students and school students can participate in this event.


Till when can I register myself?

Registration portal is open right now and would continue to do so till 31st January 2020. It is advised to register sooner than later to avoid any issues in future.

What all perks do I get by participating?

Perks include transportation, food, certificate of participation or merit, exciting prizes worth 1 Lac and fruitful learning experiences.


Will there be transport facility

Yes, for certain predetermined routes buses will be available, the detailed information to which, will be sent later.


Will the buses be available on both the days?

Yes, for the convenience of participants, buses will be there on both days.


If my payment fails, what do I do?

For any further information that you may need to know, please email us on help.navyantram@gmail.com.
