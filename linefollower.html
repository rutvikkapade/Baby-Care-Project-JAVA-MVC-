Line Follower

Problem statement :

Design an autonomous wireless robot, capable of racing on a rough track along with its fellow bots following a black line on a white background in the shortest period of time.


Team Details :

The team should not consist of more than 4 members.

Each member should belong from same college/Institution.


General Notifications :

Test arena will be provided before competition.

AC supply will be provided in the college for charging purposes.

The arena may subject to change before the commencement of any round.

The structure of the robot should not be changed during the competition.

The robot has to traverse the black line on white background.


Rules

Two runs will be given to each team. Best of two scores will be considered.

Three hand touches are allowed during each run. If you are taking hand touch you have to start from the last check point mentioned on the track where the robot has missed the line.

If you have used all the three hand touches and then robot leaves the track, that leads to disqualification of that run.

During the game play, if any part of a robot is destructed/ dismantled/ damaged the participant will be given a timeout to repair at an expense of a penalty, while the next participant can be called for the play.

Only 1 timeouts will be given for any team during each run and the participant has to repair the robot without the help from the mentor.

Only two skips per round is allowed.


Robo Specifications


The maximum dimension of the robot shall be 30 cm x 30cm x 30cm. The robot should be fully autonomous. Readymade toys car and lego bots are not allowed. The robot must have the feature to manage through different lighting conditions. The team can set the robots sensitivity before the actual run on the practice track.


Arena Specification


Track width is 2.5 cm. The track surface and course line may have unevenness. There might be abrupt angles, but these will not go below 60 degrees. There will be certain obstacles in the race track that you have to take care in design. The track length can be arbitrary and shall not be disclosed earlier. Types of hurdle may include loops, objects on track , broken line etc.


Penalties


If the robot is released from the starting box before the signal it leads to the penalty. If the robot loses the line or does not cover the line it leads to penalty. Each hand touch also leads to penalty. In case of skipping any section of line or hurdle leads to penalty.


Disqualification from game:


The robot should strictly follow the specifications provided. Any deviation from the mentioned specifications may lead to disqualification. Unethical behavior could lead to disqualification. Faculty-coordinators have all the rights to take decision for any false conduct during the event. The decision of the judges will be final and abiding. Argument with judges in any form will lead to the disqualification of the team.


Grading Policy:


Crossing each check point gives 50 points. Penalty for skipping gives -50 points Penalty for hand touch gives -25 points. Crossing the hurdle gives 100 points. Reaching to finish line without any hand-touch/skipping gives 200 Bonus points.


Winnig condition:


Winner has to complete all the race tracks in least specified time.Minimum three participant teams shall be there for a competition to take place.


Certification policies


Top two teams will be awarded by certification of excellence and cash prize for the event. All teams will be provided by certificate of participation.


all rules are subject to change**
